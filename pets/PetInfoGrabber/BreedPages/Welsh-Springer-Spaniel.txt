					The welsh springer spaniel is a superior water dog, very sharp and hard work, under any environmental conditions it is loyal and willing to work. It has a good sense of smell and can be used for any form of hunting. After a good training, can be compared with any gun dog. As a companion dog, it is the company size, larger than the cocker spaniel and strong, but smaller than English springer spaniel. It is a good guard dog, but on children is usually very mild and other animals.</td>

The back of the front legs, the hind legs above the hocks, chest and lower body are moderately feathered. The ears and tail are lightly feathered. Coat so excessive as to be an obstacle in the field should be avoided. Obvious Barber is to be avoided as well.</td>
